Co-work spaces are often seen in urban areas; however, most are not dedicated to the artistry and creative side of an individual's work life. Innovation Artist lab is a 1800 sq ft. creative facility dedicated to various individually talented artistic disciplines.

Painting, Videography, and Augmented Reality are a few creative work habitats we create. Creating high definition content and having the ability to edit and deliver it quickly, is more important than ever.

Working individually while still feeling you’re part of a team friendly environment is required for the future of the creative work environments. Providing creative tools helps our members make more use of their talents and skills to assist in turning their passions into sustainable careers in the creative field.

Many Individuals are facing the isolation of working alone at home.

Not being able to afford a dedicated space for creating income with their artistic/creative business.

We represent a not too far future where; the work and creative space you go to becomes your second home.
